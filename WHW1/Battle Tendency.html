Jojo's Part 2(Battle Tendency)

The second part, Battle Tendency, sees Jonathan's grandson Joseph Joestar crossing the world in the days leading up to the Second World War in 1938-1939 to combat the Pillar
Men, an ancient race of hostile beings responsible for the creation of the Stone Mask. Like his grandfather, Joseph undergoes strenuous Ripple training alongside
Caesar Anthonio Zeppeli, Will's grandson, under the secretive Lisa Lisa. The three Ripple users and their allies fight to protect the Red Stone of Aja, a gem sought by the
Pillar Men to complete the Stone Mask, which would grant them immunity to the sun and dominion over life itself.
